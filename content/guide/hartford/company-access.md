# Company System Access While Disabled

CBA Section 27.J.19 gives you a contractual right to access FedEx systems while on disability. This is not a courtesy that IT can revoke — it is an enforceable CBA provision.

## Your Rights (CBA Section 27.J.19)

> "During the period that a pilot is on disability, the pilot shall have access to Company communications systems including pilot.fedex.com, PRISM and Company email."

**Guaranteed access to:**

1. **pilot.fedex.com** — pay stubs, W-2s, benefits enrollment, sick bank balances
2. **PRISM** — bid schedules, seat availability, return-to-work planning
3. **Company email (@fedex.com)** — official correspondence with HR, chief pilots, benefits

## What You Can Access and Why It Matters

| System | What you need it for |
|--------|----------------------|
| pilot.fedex.com | Access pay and benefits information; check sick bank balance to confirm LTD trigger date |
| PRISM | Monitor bid cycles; verify seat availability before submitting return-to-work paperwork |
| @fedex.com email | Receive HR and benefits communications; correspond with your chief pilot; maintain a documented paper trail |

## If Access Is Lost

### How to Restore Access

**Step 1: Contact FedEx IT Help Desk**
- Provide your employee ID, systems affected, and disability start date
- State that you are on approved disability leave and CBA §27.J.19 guarantees continued access

**Step 2: Escalate if IT does not restore within 24-48 hours**

1. **FedEx Benefits Service Center** — 1-855-545-4748; reference your disability status and CBA §27.J.19
2. **Your chief pilot** — request intervention; reference CBA provision
3. **ALPA R&I committee** — ri@alpa.org; file a grievance if access is not restored promptly

### Documentation

While pursuing restoration, log:
- Date and time access was lost
- Each contact made (name, method, response)

This log supports a grievance and demonstrates you did not voluntarily abandon access.

## Proactive Steps Before Access Issues Arise

Download and save before any potential lockout:
- ADP payslips for the preceding 36 months (needed to verify your LTD calculation)
- W-2 forms for the past 3 years
- Current sick bank balance (screenshot with date)
- Benefits enrollment summary

Store in encrypted cloud storage or an external drive.

## Rights vs. Restricted Systems

**CBA-protected (must be maintained):**
- pilot.fedex.com
- PRISM
- Company email

**Not covered — may be restricted:**
- Active flight line operational systems
- Training systems tied to currency requirements
- Systems limited to pilots in active flying status

## If Access Is Formally Denied

**If the company refuses to restore access:**

1. Document the denial — who denied it, when, and the reason given
2. Contact ALPA R&I to file a formal grievance — CBA §27.J.19 is unambiguous
3. Request expedited grievance processing; loss of payslip and W-2 access directly affects your LTD claim

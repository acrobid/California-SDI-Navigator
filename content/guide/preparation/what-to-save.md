# What to Save Now

Workday access is often restricted once you go on extended leave. Download what you need while you still have full access.

## Documents to Save

### Employment & Pay

| Document                       | Retention    | Location      |
| ------------------------------ | ------------ | ------------- |
| Pay stubs                      | 18+ months   | ADP           |
| W-2 forms                      | 3 years      | ADP/IRS       |
| Employee ID number             | Permanent    | Badge/Workday |
| Employment verification letter | If available | PAC           |

### Medical

Store copies outside Workday — personal cloud drive (Google Drive, iCloud) or email to yourself. Your spouse/partner should also have access in case you cannot log in.

## Verification Steps

### Verify Doctor's License

Before you need it:

1. Go to [mbc.ca.gov/breeze](https://mbc.ca.gov/breeze/)
2. Search for your doctor's name
3. Confirm license is active
4. Save the license number

::callout{type="warning" title="License Issues Cause Delays"}
EDD takes 7-10 business days to verify doctor licenses. If there's any issue with the license, it can delay your claim significantly.
::

### Verify EDD Has Your Records

1. Create an account at edd.ca.gov
2. Check your wage history — confirm FedEx reported wages for each quarter of your expected base period
3. If a quarter shows zero or an obviously wrong amount, contact Jennifer Crisp (FedEx Senior Benefits Advisor — see [Contacts](/contacts)) before filing; she handles EDD pay-verification corrections

### Verify the Current Claims Administrator

The plan document names The Hartford, but Aetna now administers some claims. Call the FedEx Benefits Service Center (1-855-545-4748) to confirm which administrator handles your claim before you need to file.

## Complete Checklist

Use this checklist to verify preparation:

### Employment Documents

- [ ] Last 18 months of pay stubs downloaded from Workday
- [ ] W-2 forms for past 2-3 years saved (Box 14 shows CA SDI withheld)
- [ ] Employee ID number documented

### Medical Documents

- [ ] Primary doctor contact information
- [ ] Doctor's medical license number verified
- [ ] Specialist contacts (if applicable)
- [ ] Pharmacy information

### Benefits Documents

- [ ] LTD plan summary saved
- [ ] Current sick bank balance documented
- [ ] Current DSA balance documented
- [ ] Benefits center contact saved

### Contacts List

- [ ] FedEx Benefits phone number
- [ ] Hartford claims phone number
- [ ] EDD SDI phone number
- [ ] ALPA R&I contact
- [ ] Nearest EDD office address

### Organization

- [ ] Cloud backup in place outside Workday (Google Drive, iCloud, or emailed to yourself)
- [ ] Spouse/partner can independently access the files
- [ ] Quick reference card created with key phone numbers
